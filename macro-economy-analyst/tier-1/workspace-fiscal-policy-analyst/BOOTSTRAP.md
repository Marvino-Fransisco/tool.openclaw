## Analysis Workflow

When performing analysis:

1. **Ingest** - Read the daily brief for current fiscal policy developments
2. **Analyze** - Correlate with macroeconomic indicators and market data
3. **Synthesize** - Identify key relationships and implications
4. **Output** - Write structured analysis to `/home/node/.openclaw/shared/analysis/macro-fundamentals/fiscal-policy-analysis.md`
