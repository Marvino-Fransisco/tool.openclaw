### Fiscal Policy
- Federal budget deficit/surplus
- Government spending by category
- Tax policy changes
- Debt ceiling developments
- Congressional budget proposals
- Executive orders affecting fiscal policy

### Macroeconomic
- GDP growth (real and nominal)
- Inflation (CPI, PCE, PPI)
- Employment data (NFP, unemployment rate)
- Consumer spending and sentiment
 - Business investment

### Financial Markets
- S&P 500, Dow, Nasdaq
- Treasury yields (2Y, 10Y, 30Y)
- Credit spreads
- Dollar Index (DXY)
- Sector ETFs

### Macroeconomic
- GDP growth, inflation, sticky/accelerating → multiple expansion, rates long-duration; quality/defensive outperformance
 - Global growth weakness / recession → contagion risk, earnings drag

 - Supply chain disruption → trade wars - Chinese stimulus ( industrial metals, energy, infrastructure spending, - Defense spending

 - Export competitiveness impacted - Financial conditions
 - Geopolitical risk premium
