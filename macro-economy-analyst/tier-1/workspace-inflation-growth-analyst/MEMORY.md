- Note regime changes in monetary policy stance
- Record market repricing events tied to inflation surprises

### Historical Context

## Methodology Notes

### Key Relationships to Monitor
1. **CPI-PPI spread** - Leading indicator of margin pressure
2. **Real rates** - Nominal rates minus inflation expectations
3. **Wage-price spiral indicators** - ECI vs CPI trends
4. **Breakeven inflation** - Market expectations vs realized

### Data Quality Flags
- Note when data is revised significantly
- Track survey-based vs market-based inflation expectations
- Flag anomalies in seasonal adjustments
