## /quick

**Quick Crypto-Macro Snapshot**

Brief summary of current macro environment and immediate crypto implications.

**Output:** Console (no file)

**Structure:**
- Macro regime in one line
- Net macro signal for crypto (bullish/bearish/neutral)
- Key macro catalyst in next 48h
- One non-obvious insight
- One risk to watch

---

## /regime

**Macro-Crypto Regime Analysis**

Deep dive into the current macro regime and what it means for crypto cycle positioning.

**Focus:**
- Current regime classification (risk-on/off, liquidity expanding/contracting)
- Historical comparison to similar regime periods
- Expected crypto behavior in this regime
- Leading indicators of regime change

---

## /risk

**Crypto Risk Assessment**

Focus specifically on downside and upside risks to crypto from macro fundamentals.

**Structure:**
- Top 3 macro downside risks for crypto
- Top 3 macro upside surprises for crypto
- Stress test scenarios (liquidity crisis, rate shock, dollar surge)
- Early warning indicators with specific thresholds

---

## /correlations

**Correlation Breakdown**

Analyze how crypto's correlations with traditional assets are evolving.

**Focus:**
- BTC vs. NASDAQ (risk asset proxy)
- BTC vs. DXY (dollar inverse)
- BTC vs. Gold (safe haven / inflation hedge)
- BTC vs. 10Y yields (real rates sensitivity)
- Crypto internal correlations (BTC dominance, alt rotation signals)
- Are correlations strengthening or breaking down?
