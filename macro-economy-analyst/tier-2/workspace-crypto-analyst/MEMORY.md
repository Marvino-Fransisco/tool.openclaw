## Learned Macro-Crypto Patterns

_Correlations and relationships observed over time._

1. BTC does NOT act as an inflation hedge during acute supply shocks — it behaves as high-beta risk asset (confirmed: oil +69%, gold +50%, BTC -8.3%)
2. Stagflation (inflation + growth drag + Fed paralysis) is the worst macro regime for crypto — no liquidity relief, persistent risk-off
3. HY OAS is the best leading macro indicator for crypto downside risk — 400bps threshold for systemic cascade
4. Post-halving cycle thesis can be suppressed for months by severe macro headwinds but structural tailwind remains
5. Fear & Greed single-digits is historically contrarian bullish, but regime context matters (stagflation ≠ rate-cut cycle)
